My Nursing Journey

What I learned in nursing school is to build a strong personality and especially a presence of mind; you need to carry it every day. Because you're saving someone else's life. And of the most importance is being an independent person, because you're able to stand on your own when you care for a patient. I realize every subject I have taken in nursing school is very crucial because it points to someone else's life, and it was very serious when it was talked about. You need to focus and learn it anyway. You need to love your course deeply because it will lead you to be more knowledgeable than you'll expect. And I want to learn more in this course about disease, about first aid and more to save and to help someone sick. I want to be a part of people that help someone who needs help.
