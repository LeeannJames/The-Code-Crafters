What I've Learned in My GE Elective

Reflecting on my journey and the valuable lessons I've gained.

Course Overview

This GE elective has focused on coding, specifically on web development with HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. Throughout the course, I learned how to build webpages, enhance interactivity with JavaScript, and style websites using CSS. The subject not only gave me practical skills but also opened my eyes to the creative potential of coding in the digital world.

Key Learnings

Here are some of the most important lessons I've learned:

  • HTML (HyperText Markup Language) - I learned how to structure webpages using HTML, creating headings, paragraphs, lists, and links to organize content effectively.
  • CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) - I gained a strong understanding of how to style webpages, applying colors, fonts, and layouts to make them visually appealing and user-friendly.
  • JavaScript - I explored how to add interactivity to webpages using JavaScript, learning how to create dynamic elements like buttons, forms, and event-driven interactions.

Personal Reflection

This elective has expanded my technical skills and sparked a new interest in coding. I've gained a deeper understanding of how websites are built and how the combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript creates functional and beautiful webpages. This experience has inspired me to continue exploring web development and possibly pursue more advanced coding topics in the future.

Beyond the technical skills, this course has taught me valuable life lessons. I learned the importance of teamwork as we often collaborated in coding exercises, sharing knowledge and solving problems together. It reinforced how working as a team can lead to creative solutions and greater success.

Additionally, I developed a stronger sense of patience. Coding requires persistence, and there were times when things didn't go as planned, but I learned to embrace challenges and take time to debug and improve my work. This patience has translated into other areas of my life, helping me approach obstacles with a calmer and more solution-focused mindset.
